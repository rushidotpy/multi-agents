# PulseTrack – PRD (Fake)

Problem:
Busy professionals want to exercise but lack time for gym or complex plans.

Solution:
PulseTrack generates 15–30 minute personalized workouts based on:
- Available equipment (none, dumbbells, gym access)
- Current fitness level and goals
- Weekly schedule constraints

Key features:
- 3-tap workout generator (duration, equipment, goal)
- Video demos for every exercise
- Adaptive plans that adjust based on logged sessions
- Apple Health / Google Fit integration

Success metrics:
- 70% of users complete 3+ workouts per week
- Average session time: 22 minutes
- 25% week-over-week retention increase
